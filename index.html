Timestamped pins

Press N on any video page. The video pauses, you type your thought, press Enter — video resumes exactly where it left off.

Works everywhere

Any HTML5 video player, on any site. If a video plays in Chrome, Grovepin works on it.

Jump back instantly

Click any timestamp in your notes and the video seeks to that exact moment. Review your pins while the video plays.

AI summary

After 7+ pins, summarise your notes into key points with AI. 10 free summaries per month — no API key needed.

Export to Markdown

Download your notes as a clean Markdown file. Paste into Notion, Obsidian, or any note-taking app you already use.

Private by default

All notes stay on your device in local browser storage. Nothing is synced to any server. No account required.

How it works

Three steps from install to your first pinned note

1

Install the extension

Add Grovepin to Chrome from the Web Store. No account, no sign-up. Takes 10 seconds.

2

Open any video and press N

The sidebar appears on the right. Press N at any moment to pin it. The video pauses while you type, resumes when you save.

3

Review, jump, export

Click any timestamp to jump back. After 7+ pins, summarise with AI. Export your notes as Markdown anytime.
