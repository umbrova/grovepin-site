How it works

Three steps from install to your first pinned note

1

Install the extension

Add Grovepin to Chrome from the Web Store. No account, no sign-up. Takes 10 seconds.

2

Open any video and press N

The sidebar appears on the right. Press N at any moment to pin it. The video pauses while you type, resumes when you save.

3

Review, jump, export

Click any timestamp to jump back. After 7+ pins, summarise with AI. Export your notes as Markdown anytime.
